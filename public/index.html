Request Header Parser Microservice

Request a time as a parameter to this url. Either in the form of a unix timestamp like so:

https://timestamp-m-s.herokuapp.com/1450137600000

or in the form of a natural american English date:

https://timestamp-m-s.herokuapp.com/December 15, 2015

A JSON object will be returned:

{"unix":1450137600,"natural":"December 15, 2015"}
